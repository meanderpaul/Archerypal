# Privacy Policy — Archerypal

**Last updated:** June 24, 2026

Archerypal is an offline archery scorekeeping app for Android. It does not operate a backend server and does not sell user data.

## Data we collect

**We do not collect, store, or transmit personal data to our servers.** The app has no user accounts and no analytics SDK in the default build.

## Data processed on your device

- **Player names** you type for a match session
- **Scores and match history** stored locally on your device
- **Match state** shared directly between nearby devices using Google Play Services Nearby Connections
- **Camera** — used only to scan QR codes when joining a match; images are not saved or uploaded

## Permissions

The app requests Bluetooth, Wi‑Fi, location, and camera permissions because Google's Nearby Connections API requires them for peer discovery on Android. These permissions are used only for local device-to-device matching and QR scanning.

## Third parties

- **Google Play Services** — provides Nearby Connections transport on device. Subject to [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

## Children's privacy

The app is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from children.

## Contact

For privacy questions, open an issue at [github.com/meanderpaul/Archerypal](https://github.com/meanderpaul/Archerypal/issues).

## Changes

We may update this policy. The latest version is published in this repository and linked from the Google Play store listing.
